Core Capabilities

Everything You Need for Fair Hiring

Built to the exact specifications of a production-grade recruitment intelligence platform.

Multi-Dimensional Scoring

Every resume is scored across three critical axes: Skills Match (keyword density + semantic NLP), Experience Level (seniority alignment), and Culture Signals (soft-skill proxies). Scores are aggregated into a single composite rank.

Algorithmic Ranking

Deterministic, reproducible candidate ordering. Every run on the same inputs yields the same leaderboard — fully auditable.

AI Rationale

One-paragraph natural-language explanation per candidate — so every hiring decision can be justified and discussed.

Bias Detection

Automatically flags name-based demographic proxies. Score variance across name-altered profiles is tracked and reported.

Privacy-Safe Processing

PII isolation by design. Names and locations are extracted and separately tracked — never used in core scoring.

Pipeline

How It Works

A four-stage processing pipeline from upload to insights.

01

Upload & Parse

Drop your Job Description (PDF or TXT) and up to 10 applicant resumes. Our backend sanitizes file structure and extracts clean text blocks via advanced PDF parsing.

02

Semantic Analysis

Text blocks are fed into a dual-engine: semantic similarity comparison (sentence embeddings) + keyword density matching against JD requirements.

03

Score & Rank

Composite scores are computed for Skills Match, Experience Level, and Culture Signals. Candidates are deterministically sorted into the leaderboard.

04

Audit & Report

The Bias Audit module re-runs scoring with name-varied profiles, logs any score deltas, and generates a Bias Compliance Report alongside individual rationales.

Bias Audit Module

Hiring Decisions That Stand Up to Scrutiny

Our mandatory Bias Audit cell runs a controlled experiment on every screening session: it intentionally varies candidate names across otherwise identical resume profiles, re-runs the scoring model, and measures any quantitative variance.
